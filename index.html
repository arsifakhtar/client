About GIVE Gita Online Gurukul

GIVE Gita Online Gurukul is a global initiative by the Gaurannga Institute for Vedic Education (GIVE) that offers online Gita classes for kids. This unique platform provides age-appropriate spiritual education for children through authentic scriptures like the Bhagavad Gita for children, Ramayana, and other Vedic literature.

Why Should Children Learn Spiritual Values?

Material Education

Teaches how to live - focusing on skills, careers, and worldly knowledge.

Spiritual Education

Teaches why we live and what our life's purpose is - developing character and values.

Today's children are bright and capable, but are they spiritually grounded?

Let them grow with inner clarity, not just outer skills.
